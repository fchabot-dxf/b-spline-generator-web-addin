    Regrid

    Re-parameterize B-spline surface(s) in this body to a uniform N×M control-point grid. Slight shape drift is normal — least-squares fit, not exact. Rational (NURBS) surfaces are supported; weights collapse to 1 on the new grid.

    Regrid runs live as Nu / Nv / Smp change. Heavy bodies may take a beat.
